Jenny Michelle Wankhar

Handwoven Textiles

From the lush hills of Meghalaya, Jenny carries forward her family's 4-generation legacy in Eri silk weaving. Her intricate designs incorporate motifs from tribal folklore, each piece taking 2-3 weeks to complete using traditional looms.

Assam, Northeast India

Arunima Debbarma

Modern Fusion Designs

Arunima blends traditional Tripura weaving techniques with contemporary silhouettes. Her innovative approach preserves cultural heritage while appealing to modern tastes, using natural dyes and organic cotton from local farms.

Tripura, Northeast India

Lalremruati Ralte

Tribal Jewelry

From the Mizo hills, Lalremruati creates stunning jewelry using ancient techniques passed down through her tribe. Her pieces incorporate locally sourced materials like bamboo, seeds, and ethically mined stones.

Mizoram, Northeast India
